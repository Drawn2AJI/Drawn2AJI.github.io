Discover Aoshima Island

Tucked away in the tranquil Seto Inland Sea, Aoshima is a tiny island in Ehime Prefecture best known for its furry four-legged residents. Often called Cat Island, this quiet fishing village is home to a cat population that famously outnumbers its human residents.

With no hotels, shops, or cars, Aoshima offers a peaceful escape from the rush of modern life. Visitors come not for the usual tourist attractions, but for the charm of its roaming cats, scenic views, and slow, simple pace.
