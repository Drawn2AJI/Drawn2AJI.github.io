Welcome to Identity V

Step into the dark, gothic world of Identity V — NetEase’s first asymmetrical horror game. Set in a Tim Burton-esque realm of twisted fantasy and haunting memories, players take on the roles of either Hunters or Survivors in intense cat-and-mouse matches filled with mystery and skill.

Gameplay Overview

In every match, four Survivors must decode cipher machines to escape, while one Hunter hunts them down. Survivors must work together, make use of their unique skills, and stay hidden — while the Hunter uses their abilities to capture and eliminate them. The game includes a rich storyline, eerie aesthetics, and a massive roster of customizable characters and skins.
